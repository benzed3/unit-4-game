Crystals Collector



You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four different crystals below. Clicking on a crystal will add a specific amount of points to your total score/points.


You win a game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


Each time game restarts, the values of the random number and crystals will change. Good luck!
